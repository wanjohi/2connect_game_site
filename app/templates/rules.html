Game Rules

The board is 10 columns by 8 rows.
Your executable file must be name: my_ai
The game framework will execute your executable providing your player color and board layout as a comma separated string. e.g:

/path/to/my_ai r .,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,b,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,.,b,.,g,.,.,.,g,g,.,.,g,g,g,.,g
To play a move, output the color you want to play, followed by a comma, then the column you want to play (1 to 10). e.g. r,3
The game framework lets you output anything so as to store it in the log. However, the last line your ai outputs must be the move to play. e.g.

ai output: random stuff

more random stuff

r,3
The following characters represent:

Period (.) : Represents an empty spot on the board

Blue (b) : Represents a blue piece on the board

Red (r) : Represents a red piece on the board

Green (g) : Represents a green piece on the board
Each AI has 3 seconds to play a move, or it will forfeit the game.
Your AI can play either the color provided by the game framework, or Green (g). Playing any other color will result in a forfeit
